Rules

Tries:

Score:

  • You've got five (5) turns to try and guess the word.
  • A green letter means that the letter is on the correct spot.
  • A blue letter means that the letter is correct but on the wrong spot.
  • A red letter means the letter is incorrect and isn't part of the word.
  • A message wil appear if you've won or lost. A new game starts automatically.
